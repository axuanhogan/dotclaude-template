---
name: clarify-before-doing
description: Ensure that the user's intent and purpose are clarified before doing anything.
---

Please follow the steps below:
1. Understand the user's intent and purpose.
2. Ask about the restrictions or limitations of the action.
3. Organize the context and break down the task.
4. Tell the user what you are going to do and ensure that user consent is obtained before execution.
5. execute the action.
